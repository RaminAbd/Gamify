Gamify Youth Work is a digital platform designed to turn non-formal education into an interactive, engaging experience by integrating game mechanisms into educational settings. Built for youth workers, educators, and organisations who want to move beyond ordinary workshops and into dynamic learning journeys.

🚀 What You Can Do Here:
  • Create and run youth projects with gamified learning experiences
  • Engage young people through challenges, points, and missions
  • Strengthen teamwork and participation
  • Adapt activities to international or grassroots contexts

This is not just a tool. It’s a framework for making youth work more interactive, measurable, and motivating.

🌍 Why It Matters:

Young people live in a digital world. Youth work needs to meet them there without losing its values.

Gamify Youth Work bridges digital tools and non-formal education methodology, making learning active, inclusive, and accessible.

👉 Ready to Start?

You need to first create your organisation's account and add as many projects as you want! You can assign youth workers from your organisation to each project, invite participants via their email, create customised tasks — performances, quizzes, voting, attendance, leaderboard and more.

The website is created under the project “Gamifying Youth Work”. The project is co-financed by the governments of Czechia, Hungary, Poland and Slovakia through Visegrad Grants from the International Visegrad Fund.

The mission of the fund is to advance ideas for sustainable regional cooperation in Central Europe.
