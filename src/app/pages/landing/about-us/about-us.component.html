About Project

🎉 Welcome to GAMIFY platform!

😍 It is an innovative project aimed at empowering youth work organisations in promoting local and international youth projects by merging non-formal learning and gaming elements.

🤝 Gamify was initiated in 2025 with the support of VISEGRAD Foundation under the project "Gamifying Youth Work"!. It was run by organisations from Georgia, Ukraine, Poland, and Slovakia that co-created a digital gamification web platform and curricula aiming to transform youth work into a fun and educational gamified process.

👉 Since 2026, the ☂️Umbrella (a network organisation) and 🏡 Gori Youth Center jointly continued the development of the 📲 platform and, within the 🇪🇺 Erasmus+ Capacity Building in the field of Youth (CBY) program (the project Gamify Rural Youth Work (GRYW)), carried out the Gamify 2.0 version that considers improved design, score saving, mobile app version, global leaderboard, agenda builder and many more. The new version will be realised in April 2026 and will be piloted in Armenia, Cyprus, Turkiye, Ukraine and Georgia by the consortium of GRYW.
