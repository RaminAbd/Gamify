About Project

🎉 Welcome to the project "Gamifying Youth Work"!

😍 It is an innovative project aimed at empowering youth work organisations in promoting local and international youth projects by merging non-formal learning and gaming elements.

🤝 The initiative brings together organisations from Georgia, Ukraine, Poland, and Slovakia to co-create a digital gamification web platform and curricula that will transform youth work into a fun and educational gamified process.

🌍 Partner organisations involved in the project: Gori Youth Center (GYC) (Georgia) – Project Coordinator - https://cutt.ly/Ne0MFONm GYC is a youth centre initiated by Umbrella that units 5 youth centres around Georgia.. Development and Initiative (Розвиток та Ініціатива) (Ukraine) - www.developmentngo.org.ua Sempre a Frente Foundation (Fundacja Sempre a Frente) (Poland) – www.sempre.org.pl ADEL Slovakia (Slovakia) – www.adelslovakia.org

💰 The project is co-financed by the governments of Czechia, Hungary, Poland and Slovakia through Visegrad Grants from the International Visegrad Fund. The mission of the fund is to advance ideas for sustainable regional cooperation in Central Europe.

🤩 Website of the foundation: https://www.visegradfund.org/
